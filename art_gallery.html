Dawn Over Hills

Artist: A. Painter
Description: A soft-coloured landscape capturing the first light. The brushwork is loose and expressive.
Technique: Oil on canvas. Previously titled "Morning Blur" — updated title reflects the warm mood.

Urban Geometry

Artist: B. Abstract
Description: A study of lines, shadows and rhythm found in cityscapes.
Materials: Mixed media on board. This piece emphasizes contrast and repetition.

Ocean's Song

Artist: C. Shore
Description: A photograph capturing the motion of waves at high shutter speed.
Notes: Evokes sound and movement; perfect for viewers who enjoy nature photography.

(Caption: Movement frozen — yet full of energy.)

The Quiet Portrait

Artist: D. Studio
Description: A minimalist portrait that focuses on expression and posture. The muted palette helps convey introspection.

Night Lights

Artist: E. Neon
Description: A digital painting exploring reflections and color harmony.
Digital — created using a tablet and layered brushes.

How to Create a Mini Gallery (Steps)

  1. Choose 3 to 10 artworks you love.
  2. Decide on a consistent format for images (size & orientation).
  3. Write short captions and provide alt text for accessibility.
  4. Arrange the pieces in a logical flow for visitors.
  5. Share your gallery link with friends and art communities.
